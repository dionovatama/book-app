# PM Tasks

---

## Assistant PM (Junior)

> **Level**: Junior (0–2 years experience)

### 🔹 Assistant Product Manager

Your company wants to add a **Subscription / Membership** feature to the book app (free vs. paid tier, reading limits, premium content access, billing).

- **Required:**
  - Break down the subscription feature into tasks covering: free vs. paid tier logic, billing integration, and content gating
  - Assume 1 person per role (Frontend, Backend, Designer, QA)
  - Provide a time estimate for each task
  - Produce a simple timeline (table or Gantt format)
  - Deliver a shareable link (Notion, Trello, or any PM tool) to the recruiter

- **Tools you can use:** Notion, Trello, Asana, or any project management tool

- **Bonus:** Identify dependencies between tasks or add a basic milestone plan

## PM (Mid-Level)

> **Level**: Mid-Level (2–5 years experience)

### 🔹 Product Manager

Your company wants to roll out a **Subscription / Membership** feature (free vs. paid tier, reading limits, premium content, billing) and needs a full project plan before development begins.

- **Required:**
  1. **Feature Timeline** — Full task breakdown per role (Frontend, Backend, Designer, QA, DevOps) with time estimates and milestones
  2. **Risk Register** — At least 2–3 risks with likelihood, impact, and mitigation plan (e.g. billing failure, high churn, scope creep)
  3. **Stakeholder Communication Plan** — Who receives what update and at which milestone (e.g. C-level, engineering lead, marketing)
  4. **Additional Feature Timeline** — Plan at least one more related feature (e.g. expiry notification system, upgrade/downgrade flow, or referral program)
  5. Deliver a shareable doc/board link to the recruiter

- **Optional (implement at least one):**
  - Sprint/iteration structure for the subscription rollout
  - RACI matrix identifying who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed
  - Success metrics / KPIs (e.g. conversion rate, MRR, churn rate, DAU on premium)

- **Tools you can use:** Notion, Linear, Jira, or any project management tool

- **Bonus:** Pricing strategy brief, A/B test plan for the subscription page, or a dependency graph across features
